Interesting facts

favorite 1. Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world. They can weigh up to 140 pounds and grow up to 4 feet in length.

favorite 2. Capybaras have a special gland on their nose that they use to secrete a sticky substance. This substance helps them mark their territory and communicate with other capybaras.

favorite 3. Capybaras have been known to form unlikely friendships with other animals, including birds and monkeys. They have even been observed allowing birds to nest on their backs.

favorite 4. In some parts of South America, capybaras are hunted for their meat, which is considered a delicacy. However, in other parts of their range, they are protected and considered a symbol of eco-tourism.
